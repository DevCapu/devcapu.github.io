Welcome to my blog! I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and today I finally did it.

## Why a blog?

Writing is how I process what I learn. When I can explain something clearly in writing, I know I actually understand it. This blog is my way of making those notes public.

## What to expect

I'll be writing mostly about Android development — Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, architecture patterns, testing, and anything else I find interesting while building apps.

I'll try to keep posts focused and practical. No fluff.
